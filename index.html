Graphic Design and Digital Media

OWTS Graphics Dept.

The Graphic Design department at Oliver Wolcott Technical High School is a multi-layered learning experience offered through the school’s exploratory program to students entering in 9th grade.

Over four years, graphics students learn the essential skills required to excel in graphic design and digital media. Through projects, assessments and presentations, students gain deeper understanding of the Adobe Creative Suite as well as real-life production work from local businesses.
